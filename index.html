The Gospel of Jesus Christ

The word "Gospel" means "Good News." It is the glorious message of how Jesus Christ saved us from sin and death, offering eternal life freely to all who believe. Here is the scriptural way of salvation:

1

The Need for Salvation

Every human being has sinned and is separated from God. We cannot save ourselves by our own good deeds.

"For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God;"
Romans 3:23
2

The Penalty of Sin

The just punishment for sin is eternal death and separation from the holy presence of God.

"For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord."
Romans 6:23
3

The Provision: Jesus Christ

God loves us so much that He sent His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place, paying the full price for our sins, and rising again on the third day.

"But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us."
Romans 5:8
"For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life."
John 3:16
4

The Promise: Salvation by Faith

Salvation is a free gift received by faith, not earned by our works. If we confess our sins, repent, and trust in Jesus as our Lord and Savior, we are saved.

"For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God: Not of works, lest any man should boast."
Ephesians 2:8-9
"That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved."
Romans 10:9

A Prayer of Faith

"Lord Jesus, I know that I am a sinner and I need Your forgiveness. I believe that You died on the cross for my sins and rose from the dead. I turn from my sins and place my trust in You alone as my Lord and Savior. Thank You for Your grace, Your love, and the free gift of eternal life. Amen."
